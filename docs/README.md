# Power BI â€“ KPI Definitions

This document describes the key performance indicators (KPIs) visualized
in the Power BI dashboards. Metrics are described conceptually; formulas
and source data are excluded for confidentiality.

---

## Total Revenue
Represents the total monetary value generated over a selected time period.
Used for high-level performance monitoring and trend analysis.

---

## Total Units Sold
Indicates the total number of units sold in the selected period.
Used to understand volume-driven performance independent of pricing.

---

## Sessions (Traffic)
Represents user traffic or engagement volume.
Used to evaluate demand and customer reach.

---

## Conversion Rate
Measures how effectively traffic converts into sales.
Helps identify funnel efficiency and performance issues.

---

## Revenue per Session
Indicates monetization efficiency by relating revenue to traffic volume.
Useful for comparing performance across time periods and products.

---

## Daily Trend Monitoring
Time-series analysis of key metrics (sales, traffic, conversion)
to detect anomalies, seasonality, or performance shifts.
